About Us

Bosslady Salon has been a cornerstone of beauty and style in our community. What started as a small dream has grown into a premier destination for those who demand excellence in hair care and beauty services.

Our founder, Grace Bulenywa, envisioned a space where artistry meets professionalism, where every client's unique beauty is celebrated and enhanced. Today, we continue that vision with a team of passionate stylists who share her commitment to excellence.
